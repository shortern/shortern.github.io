"Shortern" (n) - Skilled and busy student that is gaining professional experience that fits in with their daily schedule.

"Shorternship" (n) - Opportunity for a Shortern to gain meaningful experience in their industry.

WE ARE REINVENTING THE MARKET FOR INTERNS

Interns are superstars, but around midterms they need a break. Shorternships are designed for active students to gain valuable work experience without needing to put their education or on campus activities at stake. The premise is simple, hire an intern for a project. Think of it like hiring a freelancer, but this is a eager to learn freelancer that is gaining valuable work experience, hopefully so they can come back post-grad and take a full time position.
