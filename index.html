"Shortern" (n) - A skilled and busy student that is gaining professional experience that fits in with their daily schedule.

"Shorternship" (n) - Opportunity for a Shortern to gain meaningful experience in their industry.

WE ARE REINVENTING THE MARKET FOR INTERNS

Interns are superstars, but around midterms they need a break. Shorternships are designed for active students to gain valuable work experience without needing to put their education or on-campus activities at stake. The premise is simple: hire an intern for a project. Think of it like hiring a freelancer, except this person is driven to gain valuable work experience.
